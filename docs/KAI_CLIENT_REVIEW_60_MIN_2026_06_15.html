Scope and invoice

What is included, and what is new scope

Invoice 2026-002 covers the KAI Next Steps Sprint. During the meeting, keep a clean line between bugs in current scope, included closeout work, and new business or product expansion.

Included closeout

  • Fix reproducible bugs in already-scoped flows.
  • Update docs and review materials to match the current app.
  • Run final smoke checks and acceptance testing.
  • Make small copy/UI adjustments from real test evidence.
  • Finish agreed safety/ops config after client decisions are supplied.

Change request or client-owned

  • New major features, paid plans, native app, or new social mechanics.
  • Marketing campaigns, school partnerships, ambassador programs, launch execution.
  • Pricing strategy and business model decisions.
  • Legal drafting and clinical program ownership.
  • Broad brand redesign after a final direction is chosen.

If something breaks during the test plan, we fix it. If something new is requested, we scope it.

Walk, jog, run

How we would pace the launch

Walk: private validation

  • Lev tests every core flow.
  • Offy names legal and clinical reviewers.
  • 3-5 trusted testers use the live walkthrough and core app flows.
  • Boost AI fixes confirmed bugs.

Jog: controlled beta

  • 15-20 invited teens.
  • Parent explanation ready.
  • Weekly bug and feedback triage.
  • No paid acquisition until retention signal exists.

Run: launch readiness

  • Final brand and content polish.
  • Final Terms and Privacy.
  • Clinical review complete.
  • Pricing, support, and launch channel owned by client.

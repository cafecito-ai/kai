HomeStreak is a chunky yellow sticker with offset shadow. Primary engine card is mint-on-cream. Every card has that pen-and-paper 2px-black-border feel. Reads warm, never sterile.

What this direction is

Warm cream base. Hand-cut sticker shapes. Chunky serif headlines + clean sans body. Solid black borders with offset shadows. Reads like a friend's well-loved zine, not a SaaS dashboard.

Palette
Cream + ink base, with peach, plum, sky, grass, sun, rose as soft pop colors.
Type
Fraunces (chunky serif, italic emphasis) for headlines and big numbers. Inter for body and labels. Punch words in italic plum.
Motion
Blob wiggle, sticker tilt on hover, gentle bounces. Less ambient, more punctuation.
Character (Kai)
A peach-colored blob with two eyes and pink blush spots. Friendly, mascot-energy, slightly silly on purpose. Anthropomorphic in a low-key way.
Copy voice
"A mentor that actually listens." "The group chat. Anything you don't feel like telling your parents." Sentence fragments. Friend-energy.
Reads as
Cash App if it was friendlier. BeReal if it grew up. Glossier if it shipped software. Distinct, warm, never tries to be cool.

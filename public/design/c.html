Palette
Warm off-white base, near-black ink, one indigo accent, one warm coral pop. Disciplined.
Type
Inter Tight at 800 for display, Instrument Serif italic for emphasis. Pairing is the entire visual identity — used everywhere consistently.
Motion
The Kai aura breathes. Subtle hover lifts. Page transitions slide. No glow, no glitter — refined motion only.

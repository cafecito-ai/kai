PROJECT NORTH STAR · DESIGN REVIEW

Three directions.
Pick the one that feels right.

Three working prototypes of Kai's home, onboarding, and meet-Kai screens. Open each one on your phone. The one that makes you go "yes" is the one we build.

Each prototype is a real working HTML page that shows the same three screens — landing, meet Kai, and home — rendered in a different visual language. Same product, same content, three different feels. This is the most important decision for the project's feel. Once we lock the direction, everything else (every screen, every animation, every word) flows from it.
